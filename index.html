About Me



Hello I am Anna Louise Jiongco and I am a UX/UI designer with a background in fine art, photography, marketing and branding. I specialize in visual content with an emphasis on photography, strategy and content design.


I enjoy finding creative and strategic solutions to solve user problems and address pain points. Furthermore, providing more empathy for the user, driving trends and innovation, and having a positive impact is what motivates me to design and create meaningful digital experiences.


My skills include Adobe Photoshop, Adobe XD, Adobe After Effects, Figma, InVision,Adobe Illustrator and Miro. I demonstrate an understanding of UX Research, UX Empathy, Visual Design, Content Strategy, Interactive Design, Web Design, Information Architecture, and UI design.
